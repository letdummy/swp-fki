Literacy Socialization

The Communication Studies Study Program, University of Muhammadiyah Surakarta (UMS) held a video game literacy socialization. The socialization which was held in Pucang Sawit Village, Surakarta was a form of community service. In this activity, the target participants are parents, because they are the first people who directly deal with their children in selecting the entertainment they enjoy.
