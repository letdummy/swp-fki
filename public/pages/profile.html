Vision & Mission

Vision

In 2029, Universitas Muhammadiyah Surakarta will become the Center for Islamic Science and Technology Education and Development and provide a direction for change.

Mission

1. Educate students to master science and technology in the field of communication and informatics based on Islamic values.
2. Conducting research activities in the field of communication and informatics, both theoretical and applied.
3. Organizing services to the community as a form of service.
4. Establish and enhance sustainable cooperation with relevant parties at regional, national and international levels.

Goal

1. To produce graduates of communication science and informatics who master science and technology in the field of communication and informatics based on Islamic values.
2. Produce research and development products in the field of communication and informatics.
3. Produce community service products in the field of communication and informatics.
4. Implement sustainable cooperation with related parties at regional, national and international levels.
